# TechnoServe Sales Pipeline Conversion Analysis

## Project Overview
Analysed 78,000+ B2B sales opportunities for TechnoServe, a SaaS company, 
to identify why their lead conversion rate dropped from 35% to 22.6% and 
recommend solutions to recover it.

## Problem Statement
TechnoServe's sales pipeline conversion rate fell by 12 percentage points 
after FY 2017-18. The business needed to understand the root causes and 
take data-driven action.

## Frameworks Used
- 5W Framework — to understand the problem
- SPIN Selling Framework — to frame interview questions
- MECE Issue Tree — to formulate hypotheses across 5 business dimensions
- Pyramid Principle — to present findings to the CEO

## Key Findings
1. Prior business relationship is the strongest predictor of conversion
   — existing clients convert at 47–83% vs 17.3% for new clients
2. Online Leads channel converts at only 6.5% vs 27.6% for Enterprise Sellers
3. Smaller deals (under 10K) convert at 39.9% but the pipeline is dominated 
   by 40K–50K deals that convert at only 11.7%

## Recommendations
1. Launch upselling programme for existing clients
2. Shift pipeline composition toward smaller faster-closing deals
3. Defund Online Leads and scale Enterprise Sellers
4. Grow Analytics pipeline — highest conversion rate but lowest volume
5. Review Legacy Modernization value proposition
